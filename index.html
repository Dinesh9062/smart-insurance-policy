Privacy Policy - Smart Insurance App

This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application "Smart Insurance" collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

What Data We Collect:

  • User's full name
  • Insurance policy details
  • Mobile number or email (if requested within the app)

How We Use the Data:

The data you provide is stored securely within the app or cloud (e.g., Firebase) and is used solely for allowing users to view and manage their insurance information later.

Data Security:

All data is securely stored on your device or cloud and is never shared with any third parties. We use encryption and secure storage practices to protect your information.

User Control:

Users can delete their stored data within the app or by uninstalling the application. If needed, you may contact us to request manual deletion.
